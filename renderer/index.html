    Output & encoding

    Clips already at the target are kept lossless; only the others are re-encoded to it. With a max file size set, the output is split into name_part1, _part2… at clip boundaries, each file under the limit.

    Background music

    Public-domain (CC0) lo-fi & chill music, fetched free from the Internet Archive — free to use in any project, no attribution required. Tracks are looped and crossfaded to fill the whole video. The footage itself is never re-encoded just to add music.
